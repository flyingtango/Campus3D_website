Data Overview

Our dataset covers the National University of Singapore(NUS) kent ridge campus and is divided into the following areas:

*Datasets of Utown and Faculty of Science will be released later.

Each structure or object has been annotated in dataset with their tags and classes. A tag is a sole attribute for an individual object containing all points belong to it. A class is assigned to each object according to its semanteme and function.
